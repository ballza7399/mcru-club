# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Admin and staff at MCRU, managing student club establishments and approving club member lists.

## Product Purpose
Manage and verify student club creation requests, member registrations, and backoffice administration.

## Brand Personality
Academic Modern (institutional yet modern, trustworthy, clean, gold and navy blue accents).

## Anti-references
Generic SaaS dashboards, excessive card nested layouts, cartoonish sketch SVGs, low-contrast text.

## Design Principles
1. Institutional respectability with modern UX.
2. High-contrast readable typography.
3. Clean, well-spaced form controls and action buttons.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA compliance, clear focus states on forms, accessible contrast ratios.
